About Page

MyNewHome is a web application designed to help new residents of cities in acclimating to their new homes. Its current form is that of a blog with helpful posts and recommendations about living in Baton Rouge, but in the future it could be transformed into an almost a TripAdvisor-like service with user ratings of local businesses and an events calendar. Target users are college students, recent graduates, and people moving for work. MyNewHome was created by 3 LSU computer science students: Benjamin Alterman, Emilia Garcia, and Pravina Pidikiti. {% endblock content %}
